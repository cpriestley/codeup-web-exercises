Banff National Park

  • It is located in Alberta, Canada.
  • The park is known for its mountains, glaciers, hot springs, and lakes, including Lake Louise and Moraine Lake.
  • It was established in 1885 as Canada's first national park.
  • The park is home to a wide variety of plant and animal life, including grizzly bears, wolves, and elk.

Yellowstone National Park

  • It is located in Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho, USA.
  • The park is known for its geysers, including Old Faithful, and its wildlife, including bison, elk, and black bears.
  • It was established in 1872 as the world's first national park.
  • The park is home to a wide variety of plant and animal life, including grizzly bears, wolves, and elk.

Yosemite National Park

  • It is located in California, USA.
  • The park is known for its granite cliffs, including El Capitan and Half Dome.
  • The park is home to a wide variety of plant and animal life, including black bears, mountain lions, and several species of deer.
  • It was established in 1890 as the first national park in the state of California.
